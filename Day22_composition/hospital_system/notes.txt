CORE ENTITIES
        Patient = patient 
        VitalSigns = composition inside a patient 
        Nurse = (aggregation) multiple nurses can be associated with patient 
        Doctor = similar to nurse 
        Medication & MedicationOrder = (aggregation) MedicationOrder uses Medication 
        Ward / Bed = patients can occupy beds and beds belong to ward 
        EDTeam/ Shift = composition of staff and equipments 


RELATIONSHIPS
        patient - vitalsigns = composition
        patient - medicationorder = composition
        MedicationOrder - Medication = aggregation
        ward - Bed = composition 
        ward - patient = aggregation 
        EDTeam - nurse/Doctor = composition
        EDTeam - equipments = aggregation
